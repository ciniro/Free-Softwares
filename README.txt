SICEP - Sistema para Controle de Estoque e Pacientes

Software developed for the management of municipal pharmacies with controlled distribution of:
1) Medicines for patients who use medicines donated by the public system.
2) Inputs distributed from a central to decentralized health units.

The software also does:
- Management of patients in a municipality
- Employee management
- Management of health units
- Inventory control

Developed in VB.NET 2005 with SQL Server Express 2005 database. It makes use of Crystal Reports.

In the system folder there are software engineering artifacts such as UML and database diagrams.
